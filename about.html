About This Project

EduHub is a premium, distraction-free learning platform designed to provide easy access to E-books, NCERT resources, and previous year papers. The goal is simple — make quality education accessible in a clean, focused, and modern interface.

This project was created and developed by Arnav. It reflects a vision of building structured, powerful, and user-friendly digital learning tools.
